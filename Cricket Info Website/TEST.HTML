FORMS OF CRICKET - TEST MATCHES

Test cricket is the longest form of cricket which lasts for a maximum of 5 days.It is considered the ultimate test of playing ability between cricketing nations.It remains the highest regarded from the game, although One Day Internationals are more popular among audience. The name Test is thought to arise from the idea that matches are a test of strength between the sides involved. These types of matches are of 5 days duration in which each team bats for two innings.There is also the follow-on system which helps the team to win a match. The first ever commercial test match commenced on 15th of March 1877,contested between England and Australia at Melbourne Cricket Club where Australia won by 45 runs.
