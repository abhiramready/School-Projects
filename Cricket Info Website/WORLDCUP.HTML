CRICKET WORLD CUP

The Cricket World Cup is an international chanpionship of one day international cricket.The championship is organised by the International Cricket Council(ICC) with a preliminary round qualification leading up to the final tournament which is held every 4 years.This tournament is world's third largest and most viewed sporting event.According to the ICC it is the most important tornament and the pinnacle of achivement in cricket.The first World Cup tournament was organised in 1975 which was won by West Indies. The Cricket World Cup is contested by test-playing and ODI-playing nations with other nations who qualify for the World Cup through the World Cup qualifiers. Australia has been the most sucessful of the five teams to win the tournament,by taking four titles.The West Indies have won twice while India,Pakistan,and Sri lanka have each won once.
